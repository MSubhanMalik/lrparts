# Product Brief

LR Parts is a specialized Land Rover parts storefront for Ireland and EU buyers.

## Goals
- Search-first experience
- Vehicle selection flow
- Category browsing
- Supplier-backed stock and price
- Product detail with specs, OEM references, and images

## Brand direction
- Premium but rugged
- Clean search and catalog UX
- Trust-focused messaging for Ireland delivery and support

## Pages
- Home
- Category listing
- Product detail
- Cart
- Checkout placeholder
- About / contact placeholders
